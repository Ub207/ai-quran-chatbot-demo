Q: What is Al-Mizan Online Quran Academy?
A: Al-Mizan Online Quran Academy is an online Quran teaching institute founded by Qari Hafiz Ubaid ur Rehman. We offer high-quality Quran education for students of all ages around the world.

Q: Who is the founder of Al-Mizan Online Quran Academy?
A: The academy was founded by Qari Hafiz Ubaid ur Rehman, a qualified Hafiz and Qari with extensive experience in teaching the Holy Quran.

Q: What courses does Al-Mizan offer?
A: Al-Mizan Online Quran Academy offers the following courses:
- Noorani Qaida (for beginners)
- Quran Reading with Tajweed
- Hifz ul Quran (Quran Memorization)
- Quran Translation (Urdu/English)
- Islamic Studies
- Duas and Surahs memorization

Q: Who can join Al-Mizan Online Quran Academy?
A: Anyone can join — children, adults, brothers and sisters of all ages. We welcome students from all over the world regardless of their current level of Quran knowledge.

Q: What is Noorani Qaida?
A: Noorani Qaida is the foundational course for learning to read the Quran. It teaches Arabic alphabets, their pronunciation, joining of letters, and basic rules of reading. It is the best starting point for complete beginners.

Q: What is Tajweed?
A: Tajweed refers to the set of rules that govern the proper pronunciation of the Quran. Learning Tajweed ensures that the Quran is recited correctly as it was revealed. Al-Mizan teaches Tajweed in a simple and systematic way.

Q: What is Hifz ul Quran?
A: Hifz ul Quran means memorizing the entire Holy Quran. A person who has memorized the Quran is called a Hafiz. Al-Mizan offers structured Hifz programs with dedicated teachers and regular revision sessions.

Q: How are the classes conducted?
A: Classes are conducted online via Zoom or Skype. Each student gets one-on-one attention from the teacher in a live session. Classes are available 7 days a week and can be scheduled at a time convenient for the student.

Q: What are the class timings?
A: We offer flexible class timings to accommodate students from different time zones around the world. Please contact us to schedule a class at your preferred time.

Q: How long is each class?
A: Each class is typically 30 minutes long. However, class duration can be adjusted based on the student's age and learning needs.
